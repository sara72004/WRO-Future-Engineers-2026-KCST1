# Phase 5 – Testing and Final Strategy

## Testing

Due to limited access to the official arena, a custom test environment was created at home using available materials.

## Observations

* Laser sensors performed consistently
* Turning accuracy improved
* Stability improved after wheel changes

## Driving Strategies

Two strategies were developed:

### 1. Stop-before-turn

* More accurate
* Reduces drift

### 2. Continuous turning

* Faster
* Slightly less accurate

## Competition Plan

Both strategies are prepared, and the final choice will be made based on performance during competition.

## Conclusion

The robot evolved from a simple color-based system into a reliable sensor-based autonomous navigation system through testing and iterative improvements.
