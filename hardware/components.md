##  Hardware Components

The robot was built using components from the **MATRIX WRO Future Innovators Set (MAFI900)** along with additional sensors and modules. The key hardware used in this project includes:

###  Main Controller
- **MATRIX Mini R4 (included in MAFI900 kit)**  
  Acts as the central control unit responsible for processing sensor data and controlling all actuators.

---

###  Sensors
- **MATRIX Laser Sensor V2 (×3)**  
  Used for distance measurement and wall detection (front, left, and right).

- **MATRIX Color Sensor V3 (MS-002V3)**  
  Used for detecting floor colors (blue/orange) to assist with navigation logic.

- **M-Vision Camera (MS-010)**  
  Used during development for visual detection and experimentation.

---

###  Actuators
- **MATRIX TT Encoder Motor (METT-MG001)**  
  Provides forward motion with controlled speed output.

- **MATRIX RC Servo with Metal Gearbox (MRC-MG996R)**  
  Controls steering of the front wheels with high torque and precision.

---

###  Mechanical Components
- **MATRIX TT Wheels (Blue Rim)**  
  Used for consistent traction and improved stability.

- **Chassis and Structural Parts (from MAFI900 kit)**  
  Includes metal beams, brackets, connectors, and mounting components used to assemble the robot structure.

---

###  Power System
- **2 Cell 18650 Battery Box (18650-HD)**  
  Provides stable power supply to the controller, sensors, and motors.

---

###  Additional Kit Contents (MAFI900)
The MATRIX WRO Future Innovators Set also includes:
- Structural metal parts (beams, plates, brackets)  
- Screws, nuts, spacers, and connectors  
- Cables (JST and Grove cables for sensor connections)  
- Mounting hardware for motors and sensors  

---

###  Custom Modifications
- **3D Printed Axle (Custom Design)**  
  Developed to fix looseness between the wheel and axle, improving steering precision and mechanical stability.
