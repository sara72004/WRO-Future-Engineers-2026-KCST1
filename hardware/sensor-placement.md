# Sensor Placement

- Front laser sensor: detects wall or obstacle in front
- Left laser sensor: measures distance to the left side
- Right laser sensor: measures distance to the right side

- Color sensor (bottom-mounted):
  The color sensor is placed facing downward toward the floor.
  It is used to detect floor colors (blue and orange) at the start of the run.

  This allows the robot to:
  - Determine the initial direction (clockwise or counterclockwise)
  - Maintain consistent navigation behavior throughout the lap

- Camera:
  Mounted above the robot to detect colored obstacle blocks (red and green)
